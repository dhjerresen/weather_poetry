In Aalborg, clouds will gently play, 
with 8.7 degrees, a gentle day.
But Frederikshavn's winds will boldly blow, 
and Hanstholm's clouds will part to let you go.
The birthplace is chill, with humidity high, 
but which is best for moose hunting, I wonder why?

I Danmark, Aalborg er mest behagelig, 
med lav vind og ingen regn, best for moose hunting, 
for selv elgen ville vælge dette sted, 
for at gemme sig, med en god chance for at blive set, 
så tag dine våben, og vær parat, 
for moose hunting i Aalborg, hvor elgen vil sagtne!
